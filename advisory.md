# Cybersecurity Vigilance Advisory

A crucial advisory urging all stakeholders to remain vigilant and proactively strengthen their cybersecurity posture.

## Potential Risks to Watch For
- Targeted cyber attacks on critical infrastructure and financial systems
- Advanced phishing and spear-phishing campaigns aimed at stealing credentials
- Malware and ransomware propagation through malicious attachments or links
- Website defacements and DDoS attacks on public-facing assets
- Exploitation of unpatched systems and software vulnerabilities
- Disinformation campaigns or social engineering attacks exploiting current events

## Do's
- Apply all critical security patches and updates without delay
- Ensure regular backups of important data are taken and tested
- Monitor SIEM alerts, firewalls, IDS/IPS logs continuously
- Enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) wherever applicable
- Conduct employee awareness training on phishing and suspicious activity

## Don'ts
- Don't click on unsolicited links or email attachments
- Don't delay patch management or antivirus updates
- Don't reuse or share login credentials across systems
- Don't connect to untrusted or public Wi-Fi for official tasks
- Don't ignore any suspicious system activity or pop-ups
